The definition of 'private'... in some way.

PornAOSP (or PAOSP) is an Android distribution focused around privacy when doing... ermmmm... certain activities.

PornAOSP is designed with simplicity and ease of use in mind. Based on LineageOS, it brings the finest stability and greatest features to the user.

Focused on improving what we see as the greatest features of Android and not sophisticating things, PornAOSP provides a smooth user experience with many intuitive customization options to satisfy your tinkering needs.

Wait... what?

What? Porn? An OS? How's it even possible?

Looks fake, huh? Let me show you how wrong you are. It's actually being developed.And you can get it here!

This project started as a joke on a Polish Redmi Note 4 users group (not available anymore). Someone managed to post a fake screenshot of PornHubOS running on a Redmi Note 4 to @rn4official. It was met with great enthusiasm, and even greater expectations. So, while it was going viral, we thought "Why not make it a reality?". And thus PornAOSP was born.

We created a Telegram group to share or project to the world, which grew to 250 members within a few hours. It also managed to run through a few of spam attacks, but that's a story for another day.

After some time we considered the issue of the PornHub trademark. We managed to release one build with PornHubOS name, but later ones will be branded PornAOSP. We strongly believe that name is much more neutral. We don't wish to risk a copyright abuse. However, the final name could change.

Features

  • Based on Android Nougat and Oreo
  • Full Substratum support
  • Pornograph - an audio player based on Phonograph
  • Pre-rooted with Magisk
  • F-Droid and AdAway included
  • Telegram Blu included
  • Lawnchair as default launcher
  • PornHub-style bootanimation
  • Custom wallpapers
  • PornHub app included!

Planned features
